Your recommended exercise plan is: 3 days of strength training and minimum of 3 days of cardio

  • Set Realistic Goals: Break your goals into smaller, achievable stages to stay motivated.
  • Be Consistent: Regular exercise is key to seeing results.
  • Start Slowly: Increase the amount you exercise by no more than 10% per week.
  • Accept Discomfort: It's normal to feel some discomfort, like shortness of breath, sweating, or aching muscles, when you first start exercising.
  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water before, during, and after exercise, especially in hot weather or during prolonged workouts.
  • Warm Up: Warming up helps prepare your body for exercise and reduces the risk of injury.
  • Track Your Progress: Keep a log of your progress in a journal or fitness app to stay accountable.
  • Alternate Activities: Try new exercises to keep your routine fresh and challenging.
  • Consider Strength Training: Strength training can improve bone health and increase overall strength.
  • Exercise in the Morning: Morning exercise can help you sleep better and maintain consistency.
